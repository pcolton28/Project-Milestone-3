Who I Am

Welcome to my website! My name is Piercston Colton, and I am a senior Integrated Health Science major with a minor in Information Technology. Born and raised in Newnan, Georgia, I am the oldest of four siblings and hold my Christian beliefs close to my heart.

My Mission

I am passionate about making a difference in my community and college campus. Through mentorship and leadership, I aim to inspire others and contribute to creating a more inclusive and supportive environment. I aspire to start my own nonprofit organization in the coming years to extend my impact even further.

My Interests

  • Education: Pursuing studies in health sciences and technology to bridge the gap between health and innovation.
  • Community Service: Active involvement in initiatives such as creating care packages for the homeless and hosting awareness events.
  • Personal Growth: Dedicated to learning, mentoring, and building strong connections with others.
  • Future Goals: Developing solutions to address health disparities and empower underserved populations.

Fun Facts About Me
